Dan Sutch started Sport England B2022 Innovation and Digital Accelerator

Visibility

Private
Public
one per line

To support Sport England and the Sports National Governing Bodies (NGBs) to achieve the primary aims and objectives of the B2022 fund, which are to:

  • Improve innovation and digital skills of Commonwealth sport NGBs.
  • Support NGBs with their amplification of sports participation opportunities, with a focus on under-represented groups and tackling inequalities.
What is the context of this work? This should make sense to someone unfamiliar with our work.

How can we accelerate the use of digital, design and data to achieve the outcomes above; develop the digital capability of NGBs and support Sports England to develop their practice in funding innovation and digital.

The overall problem this project addresses or tries to solve.

As a lead within an NBG I need direct support and help to follow user-led, test-driven practice, so that I can use digital to create a positive, inclusive legacy from B2022 As a funder in Sports England I need confidence and experience in setting the conditions to enable digital innovation amongst NGBs

Try to phrase this like a user needs statement: As a X I need Y so that I can do Z.
